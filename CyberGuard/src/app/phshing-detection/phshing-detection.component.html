Phishing Attack Detection - Stay Vigilant, Stay Secure

Phishing attacks are a form of social engineering where attackers impersonate legitimate entities to deceive individuals into providing sensitive information such as passwords, credit card numbers, or other personal data. These attacks can lead to identity theft, financial loss, and unauthorized access to sensitive information.

CyberGuard's Detection Approach:

CyberGuard employs sophisticated machine learning models, including Random Forest, to analyze network traffic and identify indicators of phishing attacks. By leveraging these powerful algorithms, our system can accurately detect phishing threats, ensuring your data and privacy remain protected.
