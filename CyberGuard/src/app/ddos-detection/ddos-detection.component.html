DDOS Detection - Stay away of Disruptions

DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service) attacks aim to overwhelm your network resources, causing service disruptions that can lead to significant downtime and loss. By flooding your systems with excessive traffic, attackers seek to exhaust bandwidth, servers, and applications, making services inaccessible to legitimate users.

CyberGuard's Detection Approach:

CyberGuard utilizes advanced Random Forest algorithms to analyze network traffic patterns and identify potential DDoS threats. By leveraging diverse decision trees, our system can accurately detect even the most sophisticated DDoS attacks, ensuring your services remain uninterrupted.
